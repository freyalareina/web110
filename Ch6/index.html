Planets

Planets are celestial bodies that orbit stars. Our solar system contains eight planets, each unique in size, composition, and atmosphere.

Stars

Stars are luminous spheres of plasma held together by gravity. They are the building blocks of galaxies and provide the energy necessary for life.

Galaxies

Galaxies are massive systems that consist of stars, stellar remnants, gas, dust, and dark matter, all bound together by gravity. Our galaxy is the Milky Way.
